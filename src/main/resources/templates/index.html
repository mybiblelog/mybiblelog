My Bible Log

MyBibleLog.com is a project to help you track and gain insights on your bible reading.

The bible is an anthology of 66 books written over a period of hundreds of years, with estimates closer to 2000 years.

It is rarely read from cover to cover, and a few of its books are read more often than others. In fact, few people have read the entire text. A 2017 survey showed that only 11% of Americans had read the whole bible.

Regardless, it remains the predominant religious text in the United States, as over 70% of Americans identify as Christians.

MyBibleLog.com is designed to help you read the entire bible, one piece at a time:

  • You don't have to read anything in order — all passages can be read independently of one another.
  • You don't have to follow a reading plan — you just need to keep reading the portions of the bible you haven't read yet.
