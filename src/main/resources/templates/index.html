MyBibleLog.com is a project to help you track and gain insights on your bible reading.

It's a fitness app for studying scripture!

Unlike regular prescriptive reading plans, which tell you what to read and when to read it, MyBibleLog.com is entirely descriptive. It tells you what you've read and when you read it.
